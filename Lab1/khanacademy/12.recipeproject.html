Fried eggs

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Oil 30 ml

Step 1: Warm up the frying pan.
Step 2: Pour the oil onto the pan. Wait until it heats up.
Step 3: Crack the eggs and put them onto the pan. Fry them for a couple minutes.
Step 4: Serve the dish.

Tonkatsu

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 1
Oil 300 ml
Pork 500 g
Panko 200 g
Flour 200 ml

Step 1: Cut the pork into 2 cm slices. Slightly beat them with a hammer.
Step 2: Coat each slice in egg, then flour and finally in panko.
Step 3: Pour the oil into deep pan. Heat it.
Step 4: Fry on each side for 3 minutes. Then use a towel to drain excessive oil.
Step 5: Cut each piece before serving.

Chicken curry

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 200 g
Oil 50 ml
Masala 40 g
Water 600 ml
Chicken 500 g

Step 1: Wash the rice. Simmer it in water in 1/1.5 proportion for about 8 minutes.
Step 2: Cut the chicken into small slices
Step 3: Pour the oil onto frying pan. Heat it.
Step 4: Fry the chicken for about 2 minutes. Add masala. Then add remaining water and simmer for around 5 minutes.
Step 5: Add the rice and cook everything on low fire for 5 minutes.
Step 6: Serve the dish.
