Travel to Sharm El Sheikh

Why do I need to visit it?

Sharm El Sheikh is a beautiful resort of Egypt, with clean water, pristine beaches, interesting culture and delicious cuisine. Here are some activities to do:

  1. Scuba diving
  2. Sunbathing
  3. Buy souvenirs
  4. Swimming
  5. Sightseeing

Food

Sharm El Sheikh has many foods to enjoy:

  1. Falafel
  2. Grilled fish
  3. Local ice cream
